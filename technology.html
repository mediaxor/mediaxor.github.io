Embedded software is any computer software which plays an integral role in the electronics with which it is supplied. It can be any code, which interacts with the hardware layer, ranging from the hardware abstraction layer, device drivers, kernel programming to application programming. Telecom, wireless products and consumer electronic products are major verticals in terms of revenue for the embedded software industry. Handheld devices, home multimedia and security systems in consumer electronic segments continue to drive momentum in the embedded multimedia industry.

Automotive

Embedded multimedia systems provide audio and video entertainment in automobiles and rich graphical user interfaces on automobile dashboards. These systems are also known as in-vehicle infotainment (IVI) systems.

Computing

Embedded computing is about executing applications - protocols, signal processing, user interface - while meeting all performance and power requirements on multi-core heterogeneous processor architectures.

Entertainment

Embedded multimedia systems provide audio and video entertainment along with rich graphical user interfaces in consumer products ranging from personal media players to bluetooth audio and mobile devices.

Wearables

Wearable technology can be put on or taken off the body. Embedded technology can be incorporated into the body using wearables, to create an intimate and convenient interface to information and technology.

Embedded Software

Growing consumer market is creating a need for localised product features, resulting in increased activity in the embedded software development domain. Multiple embedded processors in SoCs with independent functions are being adopted in electronic devices. While chip designing companies largely work on the hardware abstraction layer and device driver layer of the embedded software, they are also adapting to open source platforms such as Android, which has given a boost to the embedded software industry.

While some companies shorten time-to-market by switching to open source platforms, others prefer to grow their domain specific embedded software expertise in-house.

The embedded software industry is expanding as third party service providers are moving up the value chain to offer more high value activities relating to middleware and driver design services.

Indian vendors are investing and building competencies around new verticals which are set to grow considerably in next few years.

Multimedia in defence and space applications

Secure communications is the most widely used technology in modern defence systems. Whether it is capturing of battlefield images on ground or from satellites, these images and videos are transfered to a location for rela time processing to extract battlefield information. Communicating instructions to troops , sending messages, video and voice conferencing are very commonly used applications of multimedia in defence. In deep space explorations, multimedia applications find tremendous importance. From capturing high quality images to capturing sound in space, multimedia analysis is a potent tool for space research. Undergoing research in the area of future image predicition is beneficial for defence where they can predict battlefield changes in real time.

Multimedia in education and sports

Education is perhaps the most benefitted area of multimedia applications yet it is an active area of research. Technologies like virtual classrooms and video conferencing are making distance learning a rewarding experience. Digitization of books and content has widespread access and is an active area of research for content protection. Interactive media plays a significant role in imparting quality education to kids and adults. In sports, multimedia applications are widely used for training purposes. Technologies such as multiview recording help to review player position and forms in a 360 degree view. Such techniques have helped raised competitiveness in sports and made it more thrilling experience to watch.

Multimedia in film and music production

Entertainment is one area where multimedia applications are most widely used. The rate of consumption of multimedia data is expected to grow multifold wih the availaility of affordable gadgets and internet connectivity. Consequently, there is a growing demand for quality content and bringing real life experiences to home. Film and music production are an active area of algorithmic research to innovate in audio video effect and graphic animation algorithms.

Multimedia in industrial automation and process control

Multimedia goes a long way in automating process and quality control for different industries. Industrial robots use image processing techniques for sorting and segregating of products. Image analysis application are used for grading of food products and other commodities. Image processing techniques are used to control quality of steel and enable robotic movements in the plants.
