Sound is a major part of multimedia applications than ever before. A car is a typical example of a complex embedded audio system that integrates almost all applications of sound.

In a typical new age car - there is music and entertainment, voice activated navigation system, bluetooth connectivity with smartphone, alert sounds indicating component malfunction, etc. In fact, a car audio system hosts ten's of audio algorithms that control each of these sound related functions. With voice activated control systems, hands-free calling and VoLTE (Voice over LTE), the use of multimedia audio applications is a complex engineering solution.

Embedded audio product development is a multi stage process involving concept, research and development, audio tuning and acoustic measurements. Our current programs offer to build an understanding around these processes and look into future technologies.

EMBEDDED MULTIMEDIA ENGINEERING (AUDIO)

This program starts with a discussion about sound and audio signals. We shall discuss various components of an audio system to understand the big picture. We shall then explore how data is presented to the processor and software building blocks of an embedded audio system. Finally, we'll cover some details on how these blocks are being used in mordern operating systems such as Linux and Android.
