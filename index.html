Automotive

Embedded multimedia systems provide audio and video entertainment in automobiles and rich graphical user interfaces on automobile dashboards. These systems are also known as in-vehicle infotainment (IVI) systems.

Computing

Embedded computing is about executing applications - protocols, signal processing, user interface - while meeting all performance and power requirements on multi-core heterogeneous processor architectures.

Entertainment

Embedded multimedia systems provide audio and video entertainment along with rich graphical user interfaces in consumer products ranging from personal media players to bluetooth audio and mobile devices.

Wearables

Wearable technology can be put on or taken off the body. Embedded technology can be incorporated into the body using wearables, to create an intimate and convenient interface to information and technology.

MEDIAXOR ACADEMY

Mediaxor academy is the training division of Mediaxor Technologies Private Limited. Mediaxor technologies is established to provide product design services for the embedded software industry and is striving to build an ecosystem around embedded multimedia technologies. Embedded multimedia industry is largely driven by the need to provide customisations due to localisation, changing hardware platforms and multiple operating systems. While adaption to open-source platforms have reduced functional barriers, the major challenges are the cost pressures due to constantly evolving platforms and skill set availability.

The quickly growing pervasiveness of multimedia increases the relevance of its knowledge for today's computer science and electronics engineering students. The sigificance of multimedia engineering is not reflected in the engineering curriculum where multimedia is often studied as part of other courses like computer graphics and image processing.

Mediaxor academy specialises in imparting skills and knowledge required to advance the state of art in embedded multimedia domain. Mediaxor academy shall serve to build a community of multimedia engineers by providing trainings to students and collaborating with academia and industry, to promote multimedia related research and development activities in the community. We are just not another training institute offering programs to improve your programming skills. We are an academy, offering technology based skills and domain oriented trainings, to generate a specialised pool of talent and prepare them for industry. Our programs have been carefully designed to meet requirements of the academic curriculum and the embedded multimedia industry.
